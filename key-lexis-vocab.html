🔑 Key Lexis Vocab

Every word from the Lexis placement test, Parts Α–ΣΤ. Tap any Greek word to hear it.

The Lexis short placement test runs from absolute beginner up to advanced. Filter by part below to study at your level — Α–Γ are foundation, Δ–ΣΤ climb into opinion essays, idioms and abstract collocations.

Part Α · Basic questions

Greeting/intro Qs: Πώς σε λένε; · Από πού είσαι; · Πόσων χρονών είσαι; · free-time, drinks, plans for tomorrow.

Part Β · Articles, nouns & everyday verbs

Fill the right form: family nouns (φίλος, μητέρα, γιατρός) · adjective endings (ανοιχτός, φτηνός, καινούριος) · present & past verbs (μένω, σηκώνομαι, φτάνω, αγοράζω).

Part Γ · Connectors & structure

"Supply what's missing": relatives (όπου), comparatives (πιο ψηλός), causes & purposes (για να, επειδή) and the everyday vocab around them (λεωφορείο, μισθός, ταινία).

Part Δ · Short opinion answers

Why you learn Greek · whether foreign languages matter — λόγος, χρήσιμος, ξένη γλώσσα, εποχή.

Part Ε · Advanced transforms

Answer without negation · turn phrases into clauses · turn verbs into nouns · health/migration argument vocab (υγεία, μετανάστευση, απασχόληση, οξύνω).

Part ΣΤ · Word-formation, idioms & collocations

⭐ Make a verb from an adjective (ταχύς→επιταχύνω) · idioms (χώνω τη μύτη μου, τρώω ψωμί κι αλάτι) · abstract pairings across law, society, politics, education, work, ecology, art.
