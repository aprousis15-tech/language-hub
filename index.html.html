📚 12-Week Curriculum

Month 1 — Foundation

Alphabet · Numbers · Survival Phrases

Week 1 — Alphabet & Sounds Learn the Greek alphabet phonetically. Focus on letters you'll see on signs & menus.
🎯 Read a menu phonetically
Week 2 — Greetings & Introductions Γεια σας, με λένε… How are you, where are you from, nice to meet you.
Week 3 — Numbers & Time Count to 100, tell time, days of the week, handle prices confidently.
🎯 Handle any price transaction
Week 4 — Politeness & Survival Please, thank you, sorry, excuse me, I don't understand, can you help me?

Month 2 — Situational Fluency

Restaurants · Transport · Getting Around

Week 5 — Restaurants I Ordering food and drinks, asking what something is, dietary needs.
Week 6 — Restaurants II Paying the bill, complimenting food, asking for recommendations.
🎯 Order a full meal, end to end
Week 7 — Getting Around I Asking directions, metro/bus phrases, "where is…", "how far is…"
Week 8 — Getting Around II Taxis, airports, check-in, handling when you're lost.
🎯 Navigate transport independently

Month 3 — Connection & Polish

Small Talk · Real Conversations

Week 9 — Talking About Yourself Job, family, why you came to Greece, what you love about it.
Week 10 — Asking About Locals Their recommendations, opinions, life. Greeks love to share!
🎯 Have a real 3-min conversation
Week 11 — Compliments & Reactions Πολύ ωραίο! Expressing awe, enthusiasm, gratitude. Make people smile.
Week 12 — Final Polish Free conversation only. Handle confusion gracefully. Full italki immersion.
🏆 Trip ready!

📚 12-Week Curriculum

Month 1 — Foundation

Core Grammar · Essential Verbs · Numbers

Week 1 — Pronunciation & Basics Spanish phonetics are intuitive. Focus on rolling R, accent marks, and vowel sounds.
🎯 Introduce yourself fluently
Week 2 — Present Tense Verbs Ser vs. estar, tener, querer, ir. These unlock 80% of basic conversation.
Week 3 — Numbers, Time & Days Count, tell time, schedule, handle prices. Much easier than Greek!
Week 4 — Questions & Negation ¿Qué, cómo, dónde, cuándo, por qué? Build your full question toolkit.
🎯 Ask & answer 10 question types

Month 2 — Situational Fluency

Restaurants · Transport · Daily Life

Week 5 — Food & Ordering Restaurant vocabulary, ordering, dietary restrictions, paying the bill.
Week 6 — Getting Around Directions, transport, "¿Dónde está…?", taxi & metro phrases.
🎯 Navigate a city independently
Week 7 — Shopping & Haggling ¿Cuánto cuesta? Sizes, colors, "too expensive", bargaining confidently.
Week 8 — Past Tense Intro Preterite for key verbs. Talk about what you did, where you went.
🎯 Tell a simple story in past tense

Month 3 — Fluency Push

Small Talk · Idioms · Real Conversation

Week 9 — Talking About Yourself Work, hobbies, family, opinions. Me gusta, quiero, espero…
Week 10 — Idioms & Natural Speech Colloquial phrases that make you sound real, not textbook-perfect.
🎯 10-min free conversation
Week 11 — Future & Plans Voy a…, quiero…, planeo… Talk about your trip and future plans.
Week 12 — Full Immersion Week Phone in Spanish. Think in Spanish. All italki sessions Spanish only.
🏆 Conversationally fluent!

💬 Conversation Starter Prompts
Ask Dad to describe his childhood home in Greek — practice listening and asking follow-up questions.
Have Dad say 5 random words and you guess the meaning. Switch and you say words, he corrects your pronunciation.
Ask Dad what phrases tourists always get wrong — insider corrections are gold.
Ask him to teach you one Greek proverb and explain it. Then try to use it in a sentence.
Ask Dad to read a simple Greek text out loud and you follow along, stopping him to ask about words you don't know.
Practice the hardest sounds in Greek (γ, χ, ρ) — have Dad drill you until they sound right.
Ask Dad: "How would you actually say this in real life?" — textbook Greek vs natural spoken Greek can differ a lot.
