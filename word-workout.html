🏋️ Word Workout

Vocab that sticks — every word trained through the grammar.

Each word is trained through four stages — the order is deliberate, built on how memory actually forms:
  1. 🧠 Encode — meet the word with a memory hook (its English cousin) + its grammar "build block" (gender / perfective form / agreement forms). Linking new to known = deeper encoding.
  2. ⚙️ Grammar gym — you produce the right form (pick the article, conjugate, make it agree). Generating beats recognising.
  3. 🧱 Sentence — see it alive in a real sentence. Context is what makes a word usable, not just known.
  4. 🎯 Recall — retrieve the Greek from English, then rate yourself so it comes back at the right time (spaced repetition).
Built on four findings from memory research: elaborative encoding, the generation effect, contextual learning, and spaced retrieval. Targets your tutor's two priorities — conjugation + sentence-building, and article/noun gender agreement.
